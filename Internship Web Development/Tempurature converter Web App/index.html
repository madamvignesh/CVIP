A temperature converter is a tool or program that allows you to convert temperatures from one unit of measurement to another. Temperature can be measured in various scales, such as Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. A temperature converter helps you easily convert a temperature value from one scale to another, making it more convenient to understand and compare temperatures across different systems.
